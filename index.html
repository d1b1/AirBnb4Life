Short Term Rental Ordinance by City of Boston

Boston is looking to restrict investor unit short term rentals to 90 nights per year which would essentially shut down short term rentals by investors. These restrictions will cause the numbers of units to drop dramatically, rates to rise and will impact many local jobs, leaving house cleaners, managers, restaurant workers and local shop owners unemployed.

Read the Proposed Ordinance

Investor Unit. A Residential Unit offered as a Short-Term Rental that is not the Primary Residence of the Operator. Occupancy shall be limited to five bedrooms or ten guests in an Investor Unit, whichever is less.Section 9-14.5 D. An Operator may offer his or her Investor Unit as a Short-Term Rental for up to ninety consecutive or nonconsecutive days per year.
